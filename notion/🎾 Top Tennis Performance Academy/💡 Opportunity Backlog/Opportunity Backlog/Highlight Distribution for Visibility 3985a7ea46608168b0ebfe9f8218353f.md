# Highlight Distribution for Visibility

Description: Automate clip generation and distribution to multiple platforms (YouTube, social, college recruiting platforms) from match recordings. WSC Sports does this for leagues — the academy can replicate the workflow manually/semi-automatically using AI video tools.
Effort: Medium — depends on tooling available and match recording infrastructure being in place first.
Potential: If this works at the academy level, it's a service other Mexican clubs would pay for.
Status: Backlog
Why it matters: In Mexico there is no broadcasting or highlight DB for junior tennis. Athletes are invisible to scouts unless they create their own content.

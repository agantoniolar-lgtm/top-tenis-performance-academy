# Structured Athlete Scouting Profile

Description: A profile per athlete combining match video, performance trends, and coach notes — formatted for US college recruiters. Currently, athletes send a highlight reel assembled two months before the application deadline. This turns it into an ongoing, data-backed artifact.
Effort: Builds on match recording pipeline. Mostly curation and format design.
Potential: Could become a service for other academies in Mexico.
Status: Backlog
Why it matters: Fills the gap between "talented kid in Mexico" and "recruitable player with evidence."

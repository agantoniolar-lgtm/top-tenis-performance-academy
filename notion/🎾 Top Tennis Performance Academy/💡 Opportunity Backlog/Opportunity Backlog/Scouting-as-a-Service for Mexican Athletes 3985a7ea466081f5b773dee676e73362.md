# Scouting-as-a-Service for Mexican Athletes

Description: Expand the academy's pipeline to serve other Mexican high schools and clubs with interest in sending athletes to the US or Europe. Replaces fragmented, human-driven scouting networks with a systematic video + profile + outreach workflow.
Effort: High — requires the academy pipeline to be proven first.
Potential: This is where it becomes a business beyond the academy.
Status: Backlog
Why it matters: The academy has the proof of concept. Other clubs have the athletes but not the infrastructure.
